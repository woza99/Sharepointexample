Knowledge Hub Master Blueprint

This page maps your exact T1-T4 information architecture into clickable page templates. T1 is the single master hub site, and T2/T3/T4 are page layers beneath it.

T1: 1 Node T2: 6 Section Pages T3: 18 Categories T4: Example Content Pages Included
How to use this: Start from the T1 master hub template, then apply the T2 section page template, T3 category page template, and T4 content page template across your hierarchy. This keeps the model page-based and consistent.
🔷 T1 Template
Hub front-door, global nav owner, branding/search scope/rollups.

🔷 T2 Template
Section routing page, card links to T3 category pages, light summary modules.

🔷 T3 Template
Category page, standardized card-based routing to T4 content pages.

🔷 T4 Template
