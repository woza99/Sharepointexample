Policy Overview

This page is the controlled source for the policy summary. In a live SharePoint implementation, this layout can combine text web parts, collapsible sections, and an embedded controlled document if a PDF or Word file is the official record.

1. Purpose and Scope

This policy defines how personal data is collected, processed, stored, retained, and disposed of under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. It applies to employees, contractors, suppliers, and third parties acting on behalf of the organization.

Important: Users should always land on this page as the final destination. No additional child navigation should sit beneath T4 content pages.

2. Core Requirements

  • Process data only for lawful, documented purposes.
  • Store personal data in approved systems with controlled access.
  • Report suspected data incidents immediately.
  • Apply retention schedules and secure disposal procedures.

3. Roles and Responsibilities

Policy owners maintain the page, reviewers validate changes, and operational teams confirm process alignment. Staff are responsible for understanding the policy and completing acknowledgements when required.
